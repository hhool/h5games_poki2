DMCA Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

We respect copyright. If you believe content hosted or linked from this site infringes your copyright, please follow the procedure below to submit a DMCA takedown request. This page is intended for operators of HTML/JavaScript games and related assets.

Send a DMCA Takedown Notice

Please send a written notice by email to our designated agent at contact@poki2.online. To help us process your request quickly, include the following:

  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed (title, registration number if available, or a clear description).
  • The exact URL(s) on this site where the allegedly infringing material appears (list each URL separately).
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • Your full legal name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the owner's behalf.
  • An electronic or physical signature (typing your full name and the date in the email is acceptable).

Scope for Game Operators

For HTML/JavaScript games we host or embed, claims can concern:

  • Game code (HTML, JS, CSS) uploaded to our servers.
  • Packaged game assets (images, audio, spritesheets) served from our domain.
  • Third-party embed links or iframes that deliver the game content.

If your claim relates to assets bundled with a game, please identify the specific asset file(s) and the page or embed URL that references them.

Counter-Notice

If content has been removed and you believe this was a mistake, you may submit a counter-notice including:

  • Your name, address, and telephone number.
  • A description of the removed content and its original location on the site.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the material was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification.
  • An electronic or physical signature.

After receiving a valid counter-notice we will follow applicable law, which may include restoring the content unless the complaining party files a lawsuit.

Processing Time

We will acknowledge receipt of DMCA notices by email and act promptly. Response time may vary depending on the information provided and the complexity of the claim.
