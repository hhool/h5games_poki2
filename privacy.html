Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Poki2 ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit Poki2, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL
  • IP address (anonymized)

2.2 Locally Stored Data

We use localStorage in your browser to save:

  • Recently Played Games — A list of games you've played, stored entirely on your device. This data is never sent to our servers.

You can clear this data at any time by clicking the "Clear" button in the Recently Played section, or by clearing your browser's site data.

3. Cookies

Poki2 itself does not set cookies. However, third-party games loaded in iframes may set their own cookies. We have no control over third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective game developers' privacy policies for details.

4. Third-Party Services

Games on Poki2 are hosted on various subdomains and third-party servers. When you play a game, you may be subject to that game's own data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Poki2 does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All connections to Poki2 are encrypted via HTTPS. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Clear locally stored data at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
